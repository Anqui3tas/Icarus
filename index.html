The best way to discover

Icarus helps you find media you want to watch. With inline recommendations and suggestions, you will find yourself deeper and deeper in a rabbit hole of content you never knew you just had to see.

Media pages display organized, easy-to-digest information. We show you the information you care about seeing: RottenTomatoes scores, the cast and crew, and more.

Requesting has never been so easy

Icarus presents you with a request interface that is incredibly easy to understand and use. You can even select the exact seasons you want to watch.

A request system built for ease of use

Icarus aims to make your life more effortless than ever before. Here are some other useful features we support:

Easy Request Management

Easily keep track of what you want to watch. You can even set up automatic requests using Plex Watchlists.

Security Focused

You only need to log in once to watch. We keep your login information safe and secure using a SSO (Secure Single Sign-on).

Endless Options

Whether it is new blockbuster movies or timeless TV classics, we have got everything you need.

4K Support

Not all content is available in 4K yet, but we show the higher quality versions whenever we can.

Plex-Enhanced Experience

Our system is fully synced with Plex, so you can preview available content before making a request.

Upcoming Releases

Stay ahead of the curve by requesting future releases, available as soon as they drop.

Mobile-Friendly Experience

Use Icarus as a near-native mobile app by adding it to your home screen. Icarus is designed for use on any screen size.

Tailored Experience

Enjoy a personalized experience by choosing your preferred languages, ratings, studios, and platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there support for other languages?
Icarus has already localized into several languages thanks to the community! If your language has not been added yet, we are always accepting new translations!
Can I request upcoming titles?
Yes! Stay ahead by requesting future releases. Once available, they’ll be ready for you to watch immediately.
How customizable is my viewing experience?
You have full control! Customize what you see—from languages and ratings to specific studios and platforms—ensuring a tailored entertainment experience.
What is Plex integration?
Our Plex-enhanced experience allows you to preview available content directly within Plex before making a request, ensuring seamless access.
Is Icarus mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! Add Icarus to your home screen for a near-native app experience. It’s designed to work flawlessly on any screen size.
Does Icarus support 4K content?
While not all content is available in 4K, we prioritize higher quality versions whenever possible, ensuring a better viewing experience.

Sound awesome?

Icarus is easy to use. Simply login in with your provided Plex credentials, find the item you would like to request and follow the on-screen prompts.
